HAMPI

Hampi is renowned for being a UNESCO World Heritage Site in the state of Karnataka. This ancient village of Karnataka is popular for its ancient temples..

Monuments of Hampi

            Krishna temple complex, 
            Narasimha,
            Ganesa,
            Hemakuta group of temples, 
            Achyutaraya temple complex,
            Vitthala temple complex,
            Pattabhirama temple complex and
             Lotus Mahal complex,
